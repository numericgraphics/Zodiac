Online Ski pass

Step 1 :

Buy a prepaid card (1.50 euros). You can find them either at the ski lifts office or in the Tourist Office of Sainte Foy Tarentaise.

Step 2 :

Go to the online payment website (linked below) with your prepaid card in hand and buy a ski pass. Your prepaid card will be updated instantly.

Step 3 :

Now you can use the ski lifts and enjoy the peaks. good skiing !
